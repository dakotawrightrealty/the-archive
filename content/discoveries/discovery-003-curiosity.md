# Discovery 003 · Curiosity

## Definition

Curiosity is the pull toward what is unknown.

## Purpose

Curiosity is the seed of exploration. It begins small and scales upward from an individual action to civilization-level discovery.

## Notes

- Curiosity should be felt before it is explained.
- Curiosity should not immediately become spectacle.
- The unknown should invite movement before the reader fully understands what is being explored.

# Discovery 001 · Choice

## Definition

The first meaningful choice is the moment a conscious being realizes what comes next can be shaped by action.

## Purpose

This discovery gives the opening chapter a relatable emotional center. The first scene should not be about abstract awareness. It should be about a young conscious being facing one of its earliest meaningful possibilities.

## Notes

- The reader should understand the emotional stakes before understanding the physical stakes.
- The biological identity of the being should remain unrevealed at first.
- The choice should feel simple, but its consequences should matter.
- The choice should begin the larger movement from comfort toward possibility.

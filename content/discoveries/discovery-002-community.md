# Discovery 002 · Community

## Definition

Community is the recognition that existence is shared, and that others shape the self.

## Purpose

Community should appear before large-scale exploration. The first conscious lens should understand, feel, or participate in some form of belonging before stepping toward the unknown.

## Notes

- Community is broader than family.
- It may appear as a hive, group, colony, species network, civilization, or another form of shared existence.
- The reader should feel familiarity and belonging without needing biological explanation.

- Belonging

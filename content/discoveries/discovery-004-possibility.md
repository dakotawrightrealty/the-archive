# Discovery 004 · Possibility

## Definition

Possibility is the first recognition that reality contains more than the familiar.

## Purpose

Possibility creates the pull from comfort toward exploration. It does not need to begin as philosophy. It can begin as a simple feeling that there is more.

## Notes

- Possibility should be grounded in a moment, not explained as an idea.
- The first chapter should make possibility emotionally understandable.
- Possibility should lead naturally into choice, curiosity, and consequence.

- Curiosity

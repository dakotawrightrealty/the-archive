# The Constitution of This Universe

## Rule Zero

Protect the identity of the universe above everything else.

Exciting ideas are not automatically correct. If an idea weakens the identity of the universe, it must be revised or rejected.

## Identity

This universe explores existence through the experiences of conscious beings. Science fiction is the language of the universe, not its purpose.

Technology, alien life, space travel, cryosleep, civilizations, and cosmic scale exist to ask deeper questions about discovery, choice, connection, identity, and wonder.

## Primary Creative Lens

The project is space science fiction. Stories may follow humans, non-human beings, machine intelligences, hive minds, or forms of consciousness not yet defined. The correct lens is whichever consciousness best serves the discovery being explored.

## Core Principles

1. The universe is the main character.
2. Conscious beings are the lens through which existence is experienced.
3. Wonder is the primary emotion.
4. Discovery is more important than conflict.
5. Every answer should reveal a larger mystery.
6. Readers should empathize before they understand biology.
7. Meaningful growth begins with choice.
8. Scale is revealed gradually through discovery.
9. The unknown remains larger than the known.
10. Stories invite questions rather than preach answers.

## Reader Promise

Every story should leave the reader with a greater sense of wonder than when they began. The reader should feel more connected to consciousness in all its forms and more aware of how much remains undiscovered.

## Archive Rule

The Archive exists to serve the universe. The universe does not exist to justify building The Archive.

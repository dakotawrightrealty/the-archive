Writer’s Journal

The reasoning behind creative decisions.

Session 000 · Foundation

We defined the project as a science fiction universe exploring existence through the experiences of conscious beings. The Archive was created to protect consistency and identity.

Accepted

  • The universe is the main character.
  • Discovery is central.
  • Empathy should come before biology.
  • Choice is the first major discovery.

Rejected / Guardrails

  • Do not let technology become the point.
  • Do not make humans the default lens.
  • Do not explain every mystery.
